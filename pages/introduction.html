Gaussian estimation, MLE, MAP, and Bayesian updating

Supply a sample set, define a prior over the mean, and walk through the logic that takes you from raw observations to MLE, MAP, and posterior beliefs.

Generative learning models how the data were produced. In this toy lab, the assumption is a Gaussian data source with a prior over its mean.

      Lab 4

      Nonparametric density estimation with kernels

      Kernel density estimation does not commit to a single Gaussian. Instead it centers a little kernel on every sample and adds them together. Adjust the bandwidth and reveal the contribution of each sample step by step.

      0.45

      Where this shows up

      Density estimation supports anomaly detection, sensor modeling, speech recognition, recommendation priors, robotics state estimation, and many other generative pipelines.

        Applications

        Software tools and where these ideas fit

        Python stack

        Use NumPy, SciPy, pandas, scikit-learn, PyMC, PyTorch, or TensorFlow when you want these ideas at scale. The browser labs on this site are built to teach the mechanics first.

        Applications

        Estimators drive forecasting, medical risk modeling, industrial monitoring, adaptive filtering, finance, and scientific measurement wherever you need uncertainty-aware predictions.

        Reading the outputs

        Do not stop at the point estimate. Compare priors, sample size, noise handling, and the smoothness of the density estimate before you trust any downstream classifier.
